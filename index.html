Rachel Ciarlante

Rachel has been working in many areas of Business Administration for over 20 years. She is currently a student at Sullivan University and studying in the field of Dynamic Web Design. Her list of experience include HTML/CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Netbeans,PHP, WordPress, MySQL, Adobe Dreamweaver cc, Adobe Animate cc, Visual Studio VB, Adobe Photoshop cc, Animate cc, Adobe After Effects cc, Adobe Illustrator cc, Java, Microsoft Office, SharePoint Services, Billing, Accounts Payable, Purchasing, Scheduling, Supply Ordering, Note Taking and Transcription, Customer Service, Report Preparation, Oral and Written Communication, Expense Reports, Conference and Travel Arrangements, Front and Backend Office operations,Professional Presentations, Event planning and Problem solving.

Rachel is very organized, and detail-oriented. She excels at multi-tasking. She is an efficient, proactive, quick learner. Rachel enjoys working in a fast-paced, upbeat, positive environment in which she gets to use her skills to support others and accomplish challenging goals.
